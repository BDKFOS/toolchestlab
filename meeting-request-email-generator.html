How do you write a good meeting request email?

A good meeting request email is clear, respectful, and easy to respond to. It should explain why you want to meet, who the meeting is for, and make the next step simple by suggesting availability or a time frame.

This meeting request email generator helps you create professional emails for clients, managers, coworkers, recruiters, and other business contacts. Instead of overthinking the wording, you can generate a clean first draft in seconds.

Describe the purpose of the meeting, choose the tone you want, and the tool will generate a professional meeting request email you can send right away or edit to match your voice.

When should you send a meeting request email?

You should send a meeting request email when the topic needs discussion, a quick decision, alignment, or a real-time conversation that would be inefficient over back-and-forth messages alone.

The best meeting request emails are brief and specific. In most cases, clearly stating the purpose and offering availability is enough to help the other person say yes or suggest an alternative.
