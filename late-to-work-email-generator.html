What makes a believable excuse?

A believable excuse is simple, realistic, and appropriate for the situation. The more complex or detailed the excuse is, the less natural it can sound.

This tool helps you generate excuses for work, school, social plans, being late, missing events, or last minute situations. You can keep the tone professional, casual, or even funny depending on what you need.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a good excuse for missing work?
A good excuse is usually simple and realistic, such as illness, a family issue, transportation problems, or a scheduling conflict.

Should an excuse be detailed?
No. A short and believable explanation is usually more effective than a long or overly complicated story.

Can I use this for last minute situations?
Yes. This tool works well for last minute cancellations, being late, or unexpected situations where you need a quick explanation.
