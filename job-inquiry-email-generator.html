How do you write a good job inquiry email?

A good job inquiry email should be brief, relevant, and professional. The best messages quickly introduce who you are, explain why you are reaching out, and make a clear ask about openings or future opportunities.

This job inquiry email generator helps you create professional emails for contacting recruiters, hiring managers, founders, or companies you are interested in working with.

Describe the opportunity, choose the tone and recipient, and the tool will generate a clean job inquiry email you can send right away or edit to sound more like yourself.

When should you send a job inquiry email?

A job inquiry email makes sense when you want to express interest in a company, ask about openings, or introduce yourself before a formal application exists.

The strongest inquiry emails are short, thoughtful, and easy to answer. A clear introduction and specific reason for reaching out usually work better than a long explanation.
