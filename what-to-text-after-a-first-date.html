What should you text after a first date?

A good first-date text usually sounds relaxed, clear, and positive. It helps to show interest without overthinking it. A simple message that says you had a good time often works best.

This generator helps you send something natural after a first date, whether you want to be flirty, laid-back, or a little more direct about wanting to meet again.

Frequently Asked Questions

Should I text right away?
You can. What matters more is that the message feels natural and not overly rehearsed.

Can I hint at a second date?
Yes. A light, confident mention of seeing them again can work well if the date went well.
