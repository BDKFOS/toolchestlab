How do you ask for an update on a job application?

A good job application update email should be polite, brief, and professional. It should mention the role, reference when you applied, and ask for an update without sounding impatient.

This generator helps you write a clean follow up email to a recruiter, hiring manager, or employer when you want to check on your application status and stay on their radar.
