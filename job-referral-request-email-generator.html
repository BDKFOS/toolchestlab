How do you ask someone for a job referral by email?

A good job referral request email should be respectful, specific, and easy to respond to. It should explain the role, why you are reaching out, and make the request feel reasonable rather than pushy.

This generator helps job seekers write professional referral request emails to friends, former coworkers, alumni contacts, and professional connections.
