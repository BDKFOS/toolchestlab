How do you ask for a raise professionally by email?

A good raise request email should be respectful, clear, and confident. The best messages briefly explain why the request is reasonable, point to increased value or performance, and ask for a conversation without sounding demanding.

This raise request email generator helps you draft professional emails for salary increase conversations, compensation reviews, and role changes where your pay may need to reflect added responsibility.

Simply describe the situation, choose the tone and recipient, and the tool will generate a polished raise request email you can send right away or edit to better match your style.

When should you send a raise request email?

A raise request email makes the most sense when your responsibilities have grown, your performance is strong, or you are entering a compensation review period and want to start the conversation professionally.

In most cases, the strongest email is short and calm. A clear request for a conversation is usually more effective than trying to argue every point in one long message.
