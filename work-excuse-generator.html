How do you make a believable work excuse?

A believable work excuse should sound simple, realistic, and not overly dramatic. The more detail you add, the easier it is to make the excuse sound suspicious.

This generator helps you come up with work excuses for being late, missing a shift, needing time off, or dealing with an unexpected problem. You can keep the excuse professional or casual depending on your situation.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a good excuse for missing work?
A good excuse for missing work is usually simple and realistic, such as illness, a family issue, car trouble, or an urgent appointment.

Should a work excuse be detailed?
Usually no. A short and believable explanation is often better than giving too much information.

Can I use this for being late to work?
Yes. This tool works for being late, missing work, needing time off, or other common work situations.
