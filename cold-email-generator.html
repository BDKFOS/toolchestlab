How do you write a good cold email?

A good cold email should be short, relevant, and easy to respond to. The best messages quickly explain who you are, why you are reaching out, and why the message matters to the other person.

This cold email generator helps you create professional outreach emails for sales, partnerships, introductions, recruiting, and other first-contact situations without sounding spammy or overly generic.

Describe the situation, choose the tone and recipient, and the tool will generate a clean cold email you can send right away or edit to better match your voice.

When should you send a cold email?

A cold email makes sense when you are reaching out to someone you do not already know and you have a clear, relevant reason to contact them. Relevance matters more than length.

In most cases, the strongest cold emails are concise, personalized, and focused on one simple ask. A short message with a clear reason for reaching out usually works better than a long pitch.
