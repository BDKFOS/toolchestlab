What do you say when someone is stressed or overwhelmed?

When someone is stressed, it usually helps to sound calm, validating, and steady. The best wording often acknowledges how heavy things feel without trying to immediately solve everything.

This generator helps you come up with natural things to say when someone is overwhelmed, under pressure, emotionally maxed out, or just having a hard time.

Frequently Asked Questions

Should I try to give advice right away?
Usually not. It often helps more to validate what they are feeling first before offering suggestions.

Can I use this for work stress too?
Yes. The generated wording can work for personal stress, school stress, work stress, or general overwhelm.
