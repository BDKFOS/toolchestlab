What makes a good last minute excuse?

A good last minute excuse sounds realistic, short, and appropriate for the situation. The best excuses do not over-explain or sound dramatic unless you are intentionally going for a funny tone.

This generator helps you create last minute excuses for work, school, social plans, events, or unexpected cancellations. You can keep the excuse casual, believable, professional, or even funny depending on the situation.

If you use a last minute excuse, it usually works best when it sounds simple and natural.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a believable last minute excuse?
A believable last minute excuse is usually something simple, like not feeling well, a family issue, car trouble, or a scheduling conflict.

Should a last minute excuse be detailed?
Usually no. The more complicated the excuse sounds, the less believable it may feel.

Can I use this for canceling plans or missing an event?
Yes. This tool works for social plans, work situations, school, events, and many other last minute situations.
