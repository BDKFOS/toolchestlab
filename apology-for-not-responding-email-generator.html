Copy and Paste Apology for Not Responding Examples

Professional

Subject: Apologies for the Delayed Response Hi [Name], I apologize for not getting back to you sooner. I appreciate your patience. I wanted to follow up now regarding [topic], and I’m moving forward with [next step]. Best, [Your Name]

Short & Direct

Subject: Sorry for the Delay Hi [Name], Sorry for not responding sooner; thank you for your patience. Here’s a quick update: [insert response]. Best, [Your Name]

Client Response

Subject: Apologies for the Delay Hi [Name], Apologies for not responding sooner. I appreciate your patience. I’ve reviewed your message and will proceed with [next step]. Best, [Your Name]

Warm Tone

Subject: Thanks for Your Patience Hi [Name], Thank you for your patience; I’m sorry for the delayed reply. I wanted to follow up and say [response]. Best, [Your Name]

How do you apologize for not responding professionally?

A strong apology for not responding email should clearly acknowledge the delay, avoid over-explaining, and move directly into the response or next step.
