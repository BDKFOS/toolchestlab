How do you cancel plans politely?

A good cancellation message is usually clear, respectful, and brief. It helps to acknowledge the change without overexplaining, and to sound warm if you want to keep the relationship feeling good.

This generator helps you cancel plans in a way that sounds honest and considerate instead of flaky or harsh.

Frequently Asked Questions

Should I suggest rescheduling?
If you genuinely want to, yes. That can help the message feel more considerate.

Do I need a long explanation?
Usually no. Clear and respectful is better than giving too much detail.
