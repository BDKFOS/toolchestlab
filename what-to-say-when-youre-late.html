What should you say when you’re late?

When you are late, the best message is usually brief, clear, and respectful. It helps to acknowledge the delay, give a realistic update, and avoid overexplaining.

This generator helps you say something natural whether you are late to work, a meeting, a date, or everyday plans.

Frequently Asked Questions

Should I explain why I am late?
Only briefly if it helps. Usually the most important thing is the update, not the full story.

Can I use this for work too?
Yes. This page works for personal and professional late messages.
