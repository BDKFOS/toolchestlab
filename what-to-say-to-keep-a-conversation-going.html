How do you keep a conversation going?

Good follow-ups usually feel curious, easy to answer, and connected to what the other person just said. You do not need to force the conversation. A simple next question or comment can be enough.

This generator helps you keep conversations moving naturally without sounding repetitive, awkward, or try-hard.

Should I ask more questions?
