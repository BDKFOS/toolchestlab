How do you end a conversation politely?

A good ending usually sounds clear, warm, and natural. You do not need a complicated excuse. A simple close that signals the conversation is wrapping up usually works well.

This generator helps you end conversations without sounding rude, awkward, or harsher than you mean to.

Do I need an excuse?
Not always. A polite transition often works better than overexplaining.

Can I use this for texting too?
Yes. The wording can work for texts, casual chats, or in-person conversations.
