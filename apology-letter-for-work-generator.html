What should a work apology letter include?
A work apology letter should include a clear acknowledgment of the mistake, a sincere apology, accountability, and a brief note about how the issue will be handled moving forward.

Should an apology letter for work be formal?
Usually yes. A professional tone is best in workplace situations, especially when writing to a manager, supervisor, client, or coworker.
