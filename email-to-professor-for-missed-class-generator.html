How do you email a professor for missing class?

A good email to a professor for missing class should be respectful, brief, and clear. It should explain the absence without oversharing, acknowledge the missed class, and ask about important material or next steps when appropriate.

This generator helps students write polite emails to professors about missed class, illness, emergencies, attendance issues, and course follow-up.
