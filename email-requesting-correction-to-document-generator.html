How do you request a correction to a document by email?

A good correction request email should identify the issue clearly, mention what needs to be corrected, and keep the tone professional and constructive.

This generator helps with correction requests for reports, files, forms, proposals, contracts, drafts, and other written documents.

Frequently Asked Questions

How specific should I be when asking for a correction?
Be clear about what is wrong and what needs to be updated, but keep the email concise.

Should I sound direct or polite?
Usually both. Be specific about the correction while keeping the tone respectful.
