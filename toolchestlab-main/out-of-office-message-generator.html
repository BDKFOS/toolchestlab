What should an out of office message include?

A good out of office message should clearly say that you are away, give a rough timeline for when you will return, and mention an alternate contact if urgent help is needed.

This out of office message generator helps you quickly create professional auto-replies for vacations, business travel, sick days, leave, and other situations where you will be away from email.

Simply describe your time away, choose the tone you want, and the tool will generate a clean out of office message you can paste into your email settings right away.

When should you use an out of office email reply?

You should use an out of office reply whenever you will be away long enough that people might reasonably expect a delay. Even a short auto-reply can prevent confusion and help set expectations.

The best out of office messages are brief, clear, and helpful. In most cases, you do not need to overexplain; a simple timeline and backup contact are enough.
