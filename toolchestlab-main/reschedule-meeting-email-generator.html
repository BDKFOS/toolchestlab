How do you reschedule a meeting professionally?

A good reschedule meeting email should be clear, polite, and brief. It should acknowledge the change, apologize if appropriate, and make the next step easy by suggesting new times or asking for availability.

When should you send a reschedule meeting email?

You should send a reschedule meeting email as soon as you know the original time will no longer work. Giving early notice shows professionalism and helps the other person adjust their schedule more easily.

The best reschedule emails are short, respectful, and solution-oriented. In most cases, offering one or two alternative times makes the message easier to act on.
