What do you say when you’re nervous on a date?

When you are nervous on a date, simple and natural usually works best. You do not need to impress nonstop. Easy questions, relaxed observations, and genuine curiosity can carry the conversation further than trying too hard.

This generator helps you come up with things to say when you feel nervous, blank, or worried about awkward silence on a date.
