What do you say when a conversation dies?

When a conversation stalls, it helps to restart with something light, specific, or easy to answer. The goal is not to force energy into the chat, but to open a natural door back into it.

This generator helps you revive texts and conversations when things go quiet, whether you are chatting with someone new, a friend, or someone you are interested in.

Should I call out the awkward silence?
Usually no. Restarting naturally tends to work better than pointing out that the conversation died.
