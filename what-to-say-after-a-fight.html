What should you say after a fight?

A good message after a fight is usually calm, accountable, and focused on reconnecting. It helps to avoid defensiveness, blame, or trying to win the argument after the fact.

This generator helps you find natural wording to reopen the conversation, apologize sincerely, or make the next step feel safer and less tense.
