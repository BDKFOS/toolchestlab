What do you say when you interrupt someone?

The best response is usually quick, respectful, and not overdone. A simple acknowledgment followed by giving the other person space to continue usually works better than making a big deal out of it.

This generator helps you find natural wording when you interrupt someone and want to recover smoothly.

Should I apologize right away?
Usually yes, but briefly. A short acknowledgment is often enough.

Can I use this in work settings?
Yes. The wording can work in meetings, casual conversations, and professional environments.
