How do you apologize sincerely?

A sincere apology usually does three things: it acknowledges what happened, takes responsibility without excuses, and shows care for how the other person felt. The best apologies sound grounded and honest, not overly polished.

This generator helps you find wording that feels real, whether you are trying to repair a relationship, clear the air, or simply say you were wrong in a better way.

Frequently Asked Questions

What makes an apology sound insincere?
Defensiveness, excuses, blame-shifting, and vague wording can all weaken an apology.

Should I keep it short?
Usually yes. A short, direct apology often lands better than a long explanation.
