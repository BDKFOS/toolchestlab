How do you write a good networking email?

A good networking email should be brief, specific, and easy to respond to. The best messages introduce who you are, explain why you are reaching out, and make a simple ask without sounding demanding.

This networking email generator helps you create professional outreach emails for career conversations, warm introductions, alumni connections, industry contacts, and other networking situations.

Describe the situation, choose the tone and recipient, and the tool will generate a clean networking email you can send right away or edit to sound more like yourself.

When should you send a networking email?

A networking email makes sense when you want to build a professional connection, ask for insight, request a brief conversation, or reconnect with someone in your industry.

In most cases, the strongest networking emails are short, thoughtful, and easy to answer. A clear introduction and simple ask usually work better than a long message.
