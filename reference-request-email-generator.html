How do you ask someone to be a reference professionally?

A good reference request email should be respectful, specific, and easy to answer. The best messages briefly explain the opportunity, why you are asking that person, and what kind of reference or support you need.

This reference request email generator helps you create professional messages for job applications, promotions, school admissions, and other situations where you need a strong reference from someone who knows your work or character.

Describe the situation, choose the tone and recipient, and the tool will generate a polished email you can send immediately or edit to sound more like yourself.

When should you send a reference request email?

You should send a reference request email as soon as you know you may need a reference, especially if the other person may need time to respond or prepare. Giving someone advance notice is respectful and increases the chance of a strong yes.

In most cases, the best message is short, appreciative, and clear. A simple request with enough context is usually more effective than a long explanation.
