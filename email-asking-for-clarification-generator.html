How do you ask for clarification professionally?

A professional clarification email should be respectful, specific, and easy to understand. It should explain what part of the message, request, or instruction is unclear.

The best clarification emails sound thoughtful rather than confused. Being clear about what you need helps the other person respond faster and reduces misunderstandings.
