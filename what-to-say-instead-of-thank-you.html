What can you say instead of thank you?

Sometimes a more specific or personal phrase feels stronger than repeating thank you. The best alternatives usually name the impact the person had or why their help mattered.

This generator helps you come up with natural alternatives when you want your gratitude to sound warmer, more thoughtful, or less repetitive.
