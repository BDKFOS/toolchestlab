How do you decline a job offer professionally?

A good job offer rejection email should be respectful, appreciative, and clear. The strongest messages thank the employer for the opportunity, decline the offer directly, and avoid overexplaining.

This job offer rejection email generator helps you create professional messages that preserve the relationship while still being honest and concise. That can matter if you want to keep the door open with the company in the future.

Simply describe the situation, choose the tone and recipient, and the tool will generate a polished email you can send immediately or edit to fit your style.

When should you send a job offer rejection email?

You should send a rejection email as soon as you are sure you will not accept the offer. Prompt communication is respectful and helps the employer move forward with their hiring process.

In most cases, the best message is short, appreciative, and direct. A clear decline with a polite closing is usually enough.
