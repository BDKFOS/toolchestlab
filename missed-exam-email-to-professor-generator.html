How do you email a professor about a missed exam?

A good missed exam email to a professor should be respectful, direct, and responsible. It should explain the situation briefly, acknowledge the missed exam, and ask clearly about make-up options or next steps if appropriate.

This generator helps students write polite emails to professors about missed exams, emergencies, illness, and academic scheduling problems.
