How do you write a good check-in email?

A good check-in email should be polite, low-pressure, and easy to answer. The best messages give a little context, make the reason for the email clear, and avoid sounding overly pushy.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a check-in email?
A check-in email is a message sent to reconnect with someone, follow up on a conversation, or ask about the progress of a project or situation.

When should you send a check-in email?
Check-in emails are often sent after a few days or weeks when you have not heard back about a project, conversation, or professional discussion.

How do you write a polite check-in email?
A polite check-in email should briefly reference the previous conversation, ask for an update if needed, and keep the tone friendly and professional.
