What do you say when you hurt someone?

When you hurt someone, the best wording usually shows that you understand the impact of what happened. It helps to name your part clearly, express real remorse, and avoid shifting the focus back to your intentions.

This generator helps you say something honest and grounded when you know your words or actions caused pain.

Frequently Asked Questions

Should I explain why I did it?
Only after taking responsibility clearly. Explanations can sound like excuses if they come too early.

Is this different from a general apology?
Yes. This page is more focused on emotional hurt, impact, and repair.
