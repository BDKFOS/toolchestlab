What do you say when you’re burned out?

The best wording is usually clear, honest, and specific enough that the other person understands what is going on. It helps to focus on how things are affecting you and what kind of support or change you need.

This generator helps you find natural ways to talk about burnout without sounding vague, overly dramatic, or completely shut down.

Frequently Asked Questions

Should I say I’m burned out directly?
Sometimes yes, especially if that is the clearest and most accurate wording for what you are feeling.

Can this help at work and personally?
Yes. The wording can work in professional conversations, personal check-ins, and honest discussions with people close to you.
